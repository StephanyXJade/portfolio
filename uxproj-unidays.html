UX

UNIDAYS APP

UNiDAYS is an app and online platform that offers discounts and deals to college and university students. It provides access to various student discounts on a wide range of products and services, including clothing, electronics, food, entertainment, and more. To use UNiDAYS, students typically need to verify their student status by registering with their educational institution's email address or providing other proof of enrollment.

PROBLEM

The user has to follow different flows to get to the same place, mostly when they visit their favourites page or when they are redirected automatically to an external website.
The user is often involved in multiple steps to see what they want to see. The intention is to reduce the steps to maximum three clicks as possible making easier for the user to search, filter, browse and redeem the codes.

As the app has a pretty similar layout for all the pages, when there’s ads or content that needs to be highlighted in the reel, is barely visible and it doesn’t stands out. Using UX Copy can help the app to guide the user through the navigation process. The alignment, size and location of the elements changes constantly which can make the user flow more complex.
