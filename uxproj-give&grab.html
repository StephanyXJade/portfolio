GIVE & GRAB APP

Give & Grab connects people who want to give away items with those who want to obtain or find used items by providing a convenient platform that makes the process more efficient for both parties.

BACKGROUND

Currently, there are various ways to find free items in Vancouver. Many online users give away free items or post about items they are looking to give. Online Platforms such as Craigslist, Facebook, Freecycle Website Network, or different methods like the Community Bulletin Boards, Community Events and Reuse Centers.

PROBLEM

People want to get rid of their possessions for a variety of reasons. However, they dislike the fuss that comes with it, such as promoting items, handling tons of messages from potential takers, or paying a disposal fee for large items.
